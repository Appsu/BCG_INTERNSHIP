## BCG INTERNSHIP

## Project Overview
This repository contains work completed during the Data Science & Advanced Analytics Virtual Experience Program offered by Boston Consulting Group through Forage.The project simulates the role of a Data Scientist, where data analysis and machine learning techniques are used to understand customer behavior and predict churn.

## Project Objectives
-Understand the client’s business problem
-Analyze customer and pricing datasets
-Identify key drivers of customer churn
-Build predictive models to forecast churn risk

## Business Problem
A utility company (PowerCo) is experiencing customer churn, where customers switch to other providers.
The goal of this project is to determine whether price sensitivity is a major factor influencing customer churn and develop a model to predict customers who are likely to leave.

## Key Insights
-Price changes can influence customer churn behavior.
-Some customer segments are more sensitive to price variations.
-Predictive modeling can help identify high-risk customers in advance.
